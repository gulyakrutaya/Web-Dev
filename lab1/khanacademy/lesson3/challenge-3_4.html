Recipe of an apple pie

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
apples 7
sugar 1/2 tablespoon
flour 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Place oven rack in the center position and Preheat the oven to 400°F (204°C)

Step 2: In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice and lemon zest; toss to coat evenly.
